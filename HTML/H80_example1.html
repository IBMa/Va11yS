Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces main region, heading level 2 link, and link text for each list item.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Royal Palm hotel link, heading level 2 link.
      • Bullet, list start, Map link.
      • bullet, photos link.
      • Bullet, directions link.
      • Bullet guest reviews link.
      • Bullet, Book now link, list end.
      • Bullet list start, hotel three rivers heading level 2 link.
      • Bullet, map link.
      • Bullet, Photo link.
      • Bullet, direction link.
      • Bullet, guest reviews link.
      • Bullet, book now link, list end, end, end, main.
