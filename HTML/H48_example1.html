  1. Mix eggs and milk in a bowl.

Source: WCAG H48

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces list of 2 items and then announces the items number and text for each list item.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • 1 list start, mix eggs and milk in a bowl.
      • 2 add salt and peper list end, end, end main.
