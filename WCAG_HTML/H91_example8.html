H91 Example 8: Using HTML form controls and links

Choose a Color:

Source: WCAG H91

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces choose a color, red radio button not checked. One of three. Blue radio button not checked. Two of three. Green radio button not checked. Three of three.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Choose a color.
      • Red radio button unchecked, 1 of 3.
      • Blue radio button unchecked, 2 of 3.
      • Green radio button unchecked, 3 of 3.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • Choose a color. Tap to activate.
      • Not checked, red radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, blue radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, green radio button. Tap to toggle.
