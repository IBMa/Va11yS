H71 Example 3: Providing a description for groups of form controls using fieldset and legend elements

Your preferred philosopher

Source: WCAG H71

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces the legend, each radio button text and the status of each radio button.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Your preferred philosopher main.
      • Socrates radio button unchecked 1 of 3.
      • Plato radio button unchecked 2 of 3.
      • Aristotle radio button unchecked 3 of 3.
      • Submit button end main.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • Your preferred philosopher. Tap to activate.
      • Not checkeded, Socrates radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checkeded, Plato radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Submit button. Tap to activate.
