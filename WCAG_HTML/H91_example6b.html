  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Number one pop-up button, double tap to activate the picker.
      • Combo box selection pop-up iOS VoiceOver announces selected two.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces collapse, two, drop down list. Tap to change.
