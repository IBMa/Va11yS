H97 Example 2: Grouping related links using the nav element

  • Lemons
  • Oranges
  • Limes
  • Apples
  • Pears

Source: WCAG H97

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces site menu navigation region, list of 3 items, and the bullet link text.Also announces the related articles section and the related links.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Bullet, list start, landmark, lemons link.
      • Bullet, oranges link.
      • Bullet, limes link, list end, end, end site menu navigation.
      • Bullet, list start, landmark, apples link.
      • Bullet, Pears link, list end, end, end related links, navigation end, related articles, articles end main.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • List showing three items.
      • Lemons, tap to activate.
      • Oranges. tap to activate.
      • Limes, tap to activate.
      • List showing two items.
      • Apples, tap to activate.
      • Pears, tap to activate.
