H69 Example 3: Providing heading elements at the beginning of each section of content

Link to Cooking techniques page.

Source: WCAG H69

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces each heading level and heading in each section on the Cooking techniques page.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Cooking techniques heading 1 main landmark.
      • Cooking with oil heading level 2.
      • Cooking with butter heading level 2.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3. each heading.
    • Result: Cooking techniques, TalkBack announces
      • Cooking techniques, Tap to activate.
      • Cooking with oil. Tap to activate.
      • Cooking with butter. Tap to activate.
