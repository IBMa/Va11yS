Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces radio button checked red, one of three. Radio button not checked blue, two of three. Radio button not checked green, three of three.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces red radio button checked 1 of 3, blue radio button unchecked, 2 of 3, green radio button unchecked 3 of 3.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • Choose a color.
      • Checked red radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Unchecked blue radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Unchecked green button. Tap to activate.
