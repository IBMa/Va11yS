H85 Example 1: Using optgroup to group option elements inside a select

Source: WCAG H85

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces the labels for each optgroup when navigating the combo box selections.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • What's your favoritie food, apples, pop-up button, double tap to activate the picker.
      • Fruits, heading.
      • Selected, apples.
      • Bananas.
      • Peaches.
      • Ellipsis.
      • Vegetables heading.
      • Carrots.
      • Cucumbers.
      • Ellipsis.
      • Baked goods heading.
      • Apple pie.
      • Chocolate Cake.
      • Ellipsis.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • Collapsed, apple dropdown list. Tap to change.
      • Showing items 2 to 11 of 13.
      • Checked, apple.
      • Not checked, banans. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, peaches. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, 3 periods. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, vegetables. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, carrots. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, cucumbers. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, 3 periods. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, baked goods. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, apple pie. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, chocolate cake. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, 3 periods. Tap to toggle.
      • Note: Combox selection pop-up dialog focus on the second item (skip the first item optgroup label) and TalkBack announce optgroup label as checkbox.
