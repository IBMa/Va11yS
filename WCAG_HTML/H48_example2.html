Source: WCAG H48

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces list of 3 items, bullet and then the text for each bullet.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Bullet list start Milk.
      • Bullet eggs.
      • Bullet butter list end, end main.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • List showing 3 items.
      • Milk. Tap to activate.
      • Eggs. Tap to activate.
      • Butter. Tap to activate.
