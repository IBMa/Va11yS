H77 Example 2: Identifying the purpose of a link using link text combined with its enclosing list item

  • Tomb Raider: Legend See Images (Download Demo)
  • F.E.A.R. Extraction Point See Images (Download Demo)
  • Call of Duty 2 See Images (Download Demo)
  • Warhammer 40K See Images (Download Demo)

Source: WCAG H77

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces list of 4 items, bullet and each link in the list items.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Bullet list start, Tomb Raider Legend link, see images link, download demo link.
      • Bullet F E A R Extraction point link, see images link, download demo link.
      • Bullet Call of Duty 2 link, see images link, download demo link.
      • Bullet Warhammer 40k link, see images link, download demo link, list end, end, end main.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • Tomb Raider Legend in list, level 1, 4 items. Tap to activate. See image. Tap to activate. Download demo. Tap to activate.
      • F E A R Extraction point. Tap to activate. See image. Tap to activate. Download demo. Tap to activate.
      • Call of Duty 2. Tap to activate. See image. Tap to activate. Download demo. Tap to activate.
      • Warhammer 40k. Tap to activate. See image. Tap to activate. Download demo. Tap to activate.
