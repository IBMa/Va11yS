H48 Example 1: Using ol, ul and dl for lists or groups of links

  1. Mix eggs and milk in a bowl.
  2. Add salt and pepper.

Source: WCAG H48

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces list of 2 items and then announces the items number and text for each list item.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • 1 list start, mix eggs and milk in a bowl.
      • 2 add salt and pepper list end, end main.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • List showing two items.
      • Mix eggs and milk in a bowl. Tap to activate.
      • Add salt and pepper. Tap to ativate.
