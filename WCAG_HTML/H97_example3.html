H97 Example 3: Grouping related links using the nav element

Home About Us Products
Our Advertisers Related Links Subsidiaries

Source: WCAG H97

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces primary navigation region and the link text for each link.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Home link, primary navigation landmark.
      • About us link.
      • Products link, end primary navigation.
      • Our advertisers link, secondary navigation landmark.
      • Related links link.
      • Subsidiaries link, end secondary navigation end main.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • Primary.
      • Home, tap to activate.
      • About Us, tap to activate.
      • Products, tap to activate.
      • Secondary.
      • Our advertisers, tap to activate.
      • Related links, tap to activate.
      • >Subsidiaries, tap to activate.
