H71 Example 1: Providing a description for groups of form controls using fieldset and legend elements

The play Hamlet was written by:



Source: WCAG H71

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces the legend, each radio button text and the number of the radio button (i.e. 1 of 5).
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Hamlet Was written by, main.
      • William Shakespeare radio button checked 1 of 5.
      • Ruyard Kipling radio button unchecked 2 of 5.
      • George Bernard Shaw radio button unchecked 3 of 5.
      • Emest Hemingway radio button unchecked 4 of 5.
      • Charles Dickens radio button unchecked 5 of 5.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • Hamlet Was written by. Tap to activate.
      • Checked, William Shakespeare radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, Ruyard Kipling radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, George Bernard Shaw radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, Emest Hemingway radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Not checked, Charles Dickens radio button. Tap to toggle.
