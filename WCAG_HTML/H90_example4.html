Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces the label, required, and the state of each checkbox.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
    • I am interested in the following required, main.
    • Photograph, checkbox, unchecked.
    • Watercolor, checkbox, checked.
    • Arcylic, checkbox, checked.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • I am interested in the following required. Tap to activate.
      • Not checked, photography checkbox. Tap to toggle.
      • Checked, Watercolor check box. Tap to toggle.
      • Checked, Arcylic checkbox. Tap to toggle.
