H91 Example 7a: Using HTML form controls and links

Source: WCAG H91

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces Type your speech here. Edit, Contains text. Type text.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces type your speech here, four score and seven years ago, multi-lines text field, end main, double tap to edit.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • Type your speech here. Tap to activate.
      • Edit box, four score and seven years ago. Tap to enter text.
