H77 Example 1: Identifying the purpose of a link using link text combined with its enclosing list item

  • Check out the video report for last year's National Folk Festival.
  • Listen to the instruments
  • Guitar Man: George Golden talks about making guitars.

Source: WCAG H77

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces list of 3 items, and the text and link for each list item.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Bullet list start, check out the video report for last year's. National Folk Festival link, period.
    • Bullet listen to the instruments link.
    • Bullet Guitar Man George Golden talks about Making guitars link, period.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • National Folk Festival, in list, level 1, 3 items. Tap to activate.
      • Listen to the instruments. Tap to activate.
      • Making guitars. Tap to activate.
