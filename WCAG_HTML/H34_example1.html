H34 Example 1: Using a Unicode right-to-left mark (RLM) or left-to-right mark (LRM) to mix text direction inline

The title is "مفتاح معايير الويب!‏" in Arabic.

Source: WCAG H34

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces English text and Arabic text correctly.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
    • The tile is (English voice).
    • فتاح معايير الويب!(Arabic voice).
    • In Arabic, end main.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces the title is in Arabic. Tap to activate.
