Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces each selection, the state of the radio button and the Purchase Donuts button.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces
      • Chocolate radio button unchecked 1 of 3.
      • Cream filled radio button unchecked 2 of 3
      • Honey glazed radio button unchecked 3 of 3.
      • Purhase donuts button end, select donut end, main form end.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • No check, chololate radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • No check, cream filled radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • No check, honey glazed radio button. Tap to toggle.
      • Purchase donuts button. Tap to activate.
