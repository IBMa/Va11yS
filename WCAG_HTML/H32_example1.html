H32 Example 1: Providing submit buttons


Enter your e-mail address to subscribe to our mailing list.



Source: WCAG H32

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 45.4.0, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Text field is editable and the context does not change until the subscribe button is selected.
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: iOS VoiceOver announces.
      • Enter email address text field.
      • Subsribe botton, end main.
  • Android 7.1.2, Chrome 59.0.3071.125, Talkback 5.2.1
  • iOS 10.2.1, Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result: TalkBack announces:
      • Enter email address.
      • Enter email address edit box. Double tap to enter text.
      • Subscribe button. Double tap to activate.
  • Android 7.1.2; Pixel, Chrome 55.0.2883.91, and TalkBack Version: 5.0.4.3.
    • Result: TalkBack announces
      • Enter email address. Tap to activate.
      • Subsribe botton. Tap to activate.
