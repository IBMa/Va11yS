ARIA20 - Example 3: Using the region role to identify a region of the page


Click for weather forecast

Source: ARIA20

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 49.0.1, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screenreader announces the weather sticker region.
  • iOS 11.2.5 Safari browser is based on the iOS version, and VoiceOver on iPad
    • Result - iOS VoiceOver announces:
      • Find more about Weather in Austin, TX, link, image.
      • Weather sticker, region landmark.
      • Austin, Texas weather forecast. Actions are available.
      • Click for weather forecast, link end, weather sticker, region end, main. Get the latest forecast update. Actions are available.
