Source: ARIA2

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 49.0.1, and JAWS 17
    • Result: Screen reader announces the label, edit required, type text for text field. Announces the label of the radio group, then the label of each radio button, the state. Up or down arrows change the selection.
  • iOS 11.0 (15A372), Safari browser is based on the iOS version, VoiceOver on iPad
    • iOS VoiceOver announces:
      • Account number, text field, required. Double tap to edit.
      • Please send an alert when balance exceeeds $3000.
      • Yes, radio button, unchecked, 1 of 2, required.
      • No, radio button, unchecked, 2 of 2, required.
